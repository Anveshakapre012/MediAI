About MediAI

MediAI is an intelligent AI medical assistant platform that transforms how doctors document patient consultations and manage clinical records. Our mission is to reduce administrative burden on healthcare professionals, allowing them to focus more on patient care.

By leveraging cutting-edge AI technology, MediAI automates the transcription of doctor-patient conversations and generates structured clinical notes in real-time. This ensures accurate, comprehensive, and HIPAA-compliant medical documentation that integrates seamlessly with existing healthcare systems.
